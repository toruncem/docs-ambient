### How to record a Scribe:

![Screenshot 2025-06-10 at 4.34.04â€¯PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-06-10at4.34.04PM.png)

1. Go to the 'My Visits' tab.
2. Input the Patient Name.
3. Select your Note Template.
4. Select whether your visit is "In-Person" or "Virtual"
5. Press "Start Recording" to begin scribing.
6. When finished, select "End Scribe" to complete the scribe.

To see a more detailed tutorial, check-out [Recording](/basics/recording).
