## Accessing via the Web

1. Navigate to [ambient.commure.com](http://ambient.commure.com)
2. Create and account or log in.
3. Save hours a day.

## Installing the browser extension

Commure Ambient chrome extension is supported on Microsoft Edge and Google Chrome.

![Screenshot 2025-06-10 at 4.31.43â€¯PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-06-10at4.31.43PM.png)

1. Navigate to [**Commure Ambient - Chrome Web Store**](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/athelas-scribe/bdieablopfcbafklbkgohgfoiegjjeoo).
2. Click "Add Extension" to download.
3. Click the  icon next to the web address bar, and then click  next to "Commure Ambient".
4. To open the extension, click the Commure Ambient logo (black circle).

## Installing on a mobile device

1. Go to the App Store or Google Play Store on your iOS or Android device.
2. Search "Commure Ambient"
3. Open app called "Commure Ambient"
4. Download the app
