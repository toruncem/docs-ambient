1. Navigate to [ambient.commure.com](http://ambient.commure.com)

## Installing the browser extension

Commure Ambient chrome extension is supported on Microsoft Edge and Google Chrome.

1. Navigate to [**Commure Ambient - Chrome Web Store**](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/athelas-scribe/bdieablopfcbafklbkgohgfoiegjjeoo).
2. Click "Add Extension" to download.
3. Click the  icon next to the web address bar, and then click  next to "Commure Ambient".
4. To open the extension, click the Commure Ambient logo (black circle).

## Installing on a mobile device

1. Go to the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/commure-ambient-scribemobile/id6449020492) or [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.athelas.scribe&pcampaignid=web_share) on your iOS or Android device.
2. Search "Commure Ambient"
3. Open app called "Commure Ambient"
4. Download the app

## Enable Microphone Permissions

If you are using the web app or the Chrome extension and are unable to start recording, you will need to mannually allow microphone permission

Website: 

1. Click on the website settings located in the top left corner
2. Toggle the bar to enable microphone permission

Chrome Extension:

1. Go to extension settings by right clicking the extension
2. Click on "View Web Permissions", this will open a new tab with all the permissions
3. Press "Allow" microphone permission
