## Installation

1. Download the Commure Dictation installer from your organization's software portal or the link provided by your administrator.
2. Run the installer and follow the on-screen prompts.
3. Launch Commure Dictation from your Start menu or desktop shortcut.

## First-Time Login

Commure Dictation uses your existing Commure account. If you already use Commure Ambient, sign in with the same credentials.

1. Enter your email address.
2. Complete the verification process.
3. You're ready to dictate.

## Selecting Your Microphone

1. Click the microphone indicator in the top-right corner of the home screen.
2. Select your preferred microphone from the list of available devices.
3. Speak briefly to confirm the audio level indicator responds.
