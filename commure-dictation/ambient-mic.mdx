title: "Ambient Mic"
---

Commure Ambient allows you to use your **phone as a wireless microphone** for dictation — no external hardware required. Instead of using a Powermic or SpeechMike, you can dictate directly into the **Commure Desktop App** using the **Commure Ambient mobile app**. You can:

- Use your phone as a real-time dictation microphone
- Insert text directly at your desktop's cursor in the EHR
- Access shortcuts and voice commands
- View and trigger macros
- Record from anywhere in the room without being tethered to a device

# Set Up Instructions

1. Open the **Commure Desktop App** on your Windows computer.
2. Open the **Commure Ambient app** on your phone.
3. Navigate to the **Dictation** tab in the mobile app.
4. Ensure both devices are:
   - On the same WiFi network
   - Logged in with the same email

Once connected, you will see the mobile device indicate that it is paired with your desktop.

If you do not see a connection:

- Confirm both devices are on the same WiFi
- Confirm the desktop app is open
- Log out and back in on both devices if needed

# Using the Dictation Tab

The **Dictation** tab on mobile is your control center for Mobile Mic.

From here, you can:

### 🎙 Record

Tap the record button to begin dictating.\
Text will insert wherever your cursor is placed in the EHR or notepad.
