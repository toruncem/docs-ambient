title: "Chrome Extension Integration"
---

Some EHRs are only supported via the Chrome Extension integration. For this method, the scribe is not linked to any appointment - rather, you are only required to be on any empty patient note in your EHR before syncing.

#### **How it works:**

1. Open the patient's chart in your EHR and navigate to where you would normally document (e.g., the note or encounter field).
2. On that **same tab**, open the [Commure Ambient Chrome Extension](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/commure-ambient-formerly/bdieablopfcbafklbkgohgfoiegjjeoo).
3. Either record a new scribe under **Visits**, or navigate to your generated scribe under **Scribes**. 
4. Press the **EHR** button in the Chrome Extension to begin the sync.
   > ⚠️ **Important:** The Chrome Extension needs to detect the text field it will write back to. If you're on the wrong page, it will raise an error. Make sure you're on the correct documentation page before syncing.
5. **Leave your mouse alone** - do not click or multitask while the sync is running. It only takes a few seconds.
6. Once complete, review the note in your EHR.

If you sync to the same note more than once, the content will **not** overwrite what's already there - it will **append** underneath. For this reason, we recommend doing all of your editing inside Commure Ambient (using our AI editing tools) and only syncing to the EHR when you're satisfied with the note.
