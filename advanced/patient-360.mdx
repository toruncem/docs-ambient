The Commure Assistant lets providers ask natural-language questions about any of this patient context, turning chart review into a conversation.

### Pre-visit Summary

- Automatically generated for upcoming appointments with available EHR data.
- Surfaces various sections: `At A Glance`, `Last Visit Summary`, `Active Problems`, `Recent Labs`, `Recent Imaging and Diagnostics`, `Active Meds`, and `Allergies`.
- Each section shows relevant data or an empty state indicating data is not on file.

---

### Commure Assistant Tab (Search & Summarization)

Providers can ask questions grounded in the patient's EHR data.

**Example queries:**

- "Does the patient have any allergies?"
- "Does this patient have any recent lab results?"
- "Summarize the last visit."

<Note>
  Answers are generated from the patient's available EHR data. If data is missing or incomplete, the Assistant will indicate that information is not on file.
</Note>

## Sources 

The Patient360 interface includes a dedicated Sources tab, giving clinicians a structured, navigable index of the patient's integrated EHR data directly alongside the AI-generated pre-visit summary. The Sources tab brings full transparency to the Patient360 experience before a recording ever begins.

- **Structured EHR index:** Clinicians can browse patient data organized by category—including previous visit notes, lab results, active medications, and allergies—without leaving the Patient360 view.
- **Click-through navigation:** Selecting any category surfaces the underlying records immediately, enabling quick verification of AI-generated summaries against the source data.

### Known Limitations

- Currently supporting **Athena EHR only**.
- Does not work on Safari.
- UI may be impacted on small screen Android devices.
