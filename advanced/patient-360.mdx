title: "[New] Patient 360 "
---

Patient360 gives providers a comprehensive AI-generated pre-visit summary powered by EHR data, surfacing medications, labs, allergies, and past signed notes before the appointment begins. The Commure Assistant lets providers ask natural-language questions about any of this patient context, turning chart review into a conversation.

![Image](/images/image-18.png)

Pre-visit summary view showing EHR sections on the Visits tab

### Pre-visit Summary

- Automatically generated for upcoming appointments with available EHR data.
- Surfaces various sections: `At A Glance`, `Last Visit Summary`, `Active Problems`, `Recent Labs`, `Recent Imaging and Diagnostics`, `Active Meds`, and `Allergies`.
- Each section shows relevant data or an empty state indicating data is not on file.

### Commure Assistant (Search & Summarization)

- Providers can ask questions grounded in the patient's EHR data.
- Example queries:
  - "Does the patient have any allergies?"
  - "Does this patient have any recent lab results?"
  - "Summarize the last visit."

**Note:** Answers are generated from the patient's available EHR data. If data is missing or incomplete, the Assistant will indicate that information is not on file.

### Known Limitations

- Currently supporting **Athena EHR only**.
- Does not work on Safari.
- UI may be impacted on small screen Android devices.
