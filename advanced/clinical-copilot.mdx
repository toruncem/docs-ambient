## Commure Assistant

**Commure Assistant** is your AI-powered assistant inside Commure Ambient. It allows you to ask medical questions, get help with documentation, and quickly find information—all without leaving the app.

---

### What Can Commure Assistant Do?

You can use Commure Assistant to:

- Ask general **medical questions** (e.g., “What are the common symptoms of strep throat?”)
- Get **drug interaction** guidance
- Ask for **explanations** of medical terms
- Request **summaries** or insights about the current scribe/note
- Clarify clinical content in a structured format 

**Scribe-based** Commure Assistant will use context from within the generated note to answer asked questions and prompts. 

---

### How to Use Commure Assistant

1. Tap the **Commure Assistant** icon on the main bar.
   - If you launch **Commure Assistant** from within a generated note (the button will appear in the bottom right-hand corner of the scribe), it will have full context on the scribe and the patient visit. 
2. Type your question in the “Ask a medical question” box.
3. Choose from helpful quick-suggestion tags like:
   - `Common symptoms`
   - `Drug interactions`
   - `Explain terms`
4. Press the ➤ send icon to submit.

Commure Assistant responds instantly with relevant information powered by GPT-4o.
