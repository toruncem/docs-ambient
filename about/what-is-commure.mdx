## About Commure

Commure is a leading healthcare technology company dedicated to transforming clinical documentation and improving the healthcare experience for both providers and patients. Founded with a mission to reduce administrative burden and enable clinicians to focus on what matters most—patient care—Commure develops innovative AI-powered solutions that streamline workflows, enhance documentation quality, and drive better clinical outcomes.

## Our Mission

At Commure, we believe that healthcare providers should spend their time caring for patients, not filling out paperwork. Our mission is to leverage cutting-edge artificial intelligence and machine learning technologies to automate and enhance clinical documentation, reducing the time clinicians spend on administrative tasks while improving the quality and accuracy of medical records.

## Core Products

### Commure Ambient

**Commure Ambient** is our flagship ambient AI scribing solution that revolutionizes how healthcare providers document patient encounters. Using advanced speech recognition, natural language processing, and large language models, Ambient captures and transcribes provider-patient conversations in real-time, automatically generating structured, EHR-ready clinical notes.

Key features include:
- **Real-time transcription** with support for 60+ languages, accents, and dialects
- **Intelligent note generation** using specialty-tuned AI models
- **Autonomous coding** for CPT, ICD-10, and quality metrics
- **Deep EHR integration** with major systems like Epic, Athenahealth, and eClinicalWorks
- **AI-powered clinical insights** and care nudges
- **Multi-platform access** via mobile apps, web browser, and Chrome extension

### Commure Dictation

**Commure Dictation** provides powerful voice-to-text dictation capabilities integrated directly into EHR systems. It offers healthcare providers a fast, accurate way to document patient encounters using natural speech, with support for voice commands, macros, and custom formatting.
